BorderLogix Core – Terms of Use

License to Use: BorderLogix grants the User a non-exclusive, non-transferable license to access the platform for preparing Customs declarations.

User Responsibility: The User is the "Declarant" under the PNG Customs Act. BorderLogix is a tool, not a broker. The User must verify all data before lodgement.

Prohibited Acts: Users shall not attempt to reverse-engineer the HS Classification logic or use the platform to facilitate illicit trade.

Subscription & Payment: Access is contingent upon timely payment. Failure to pay will result in the suspension of the XML generation features and "Locking" of the audit archive.

Termination: BorderLogix reserves the right to terminate any account found to be intentionally inputting fraudulent data.

BorderLogix Core – Privacy Policy

Data Collection: We collect company identifiers (TIN), transaction metadata, and uploaded trade documents (Invoices/BLs).

Purpose of Use: Data is used solely to generate ASYCUDA-compliant XMLs and provide an audit trail for the User.

Data Sharing: We never sell trade data to third parties. Data is only shared with PNG Customs if the User initiates a "Secure Audit Link" or if required by a court order.

Security: We utilize industry-standard AES-256 encryption. Our staff cannot view your commercial invoice values without a specific support ticket authorization from the User.

Retention: Data is stored for the statutory period required by the Customs Act (usually 5-7 years) unless the User requests earlier deletion (subject to legal requirements).

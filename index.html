About

Proudly PNG-owned and based in Port Moresby, BorderLogix is a premier provider of cross-border solutions, driving efficiency for economic operators navigating PNG borders.

Established in 2021, right in the middle of the pandemic, with a mission to make trade easier and more reliable. With more than two decades of experience and strong connections across the country, we work directly with border agencies to keep your business in step with the WCO SAFE framework and PNG Customs Act and relevant legislation.

Our cutting-edge Single Window trade PNG platform streamlines operations for local, international, and e-commerce traders. By utilizing automated reconciliation, we eliminate audit mis-classifications and ensure rapid, accurate transactions that meet the highest regulatory benchmarks.

What is Drawback?

It is a refund (typically 99%) of customs duties, taxes, and fees paid on imported goods that are later exported or destroyed.

Key Drawback Solutions We Provide:

01 Manufacturing Drawback

For: Companies manufacturing within PNG SEZs.

Recover duties paid on all imported inputs (raw materials, components) used in exported finished goods.

02 Rejected Merchandise Drawback

For: Defective or non-conforming imports.

Secure a refund of duties paid on the original import, even if the goods were not used in production.

03 Same Condition Drawback

For: Unused resale or return goods.

Claim duties on commercial exports that have been exported in the same state as they were imported.
